Soma Suzuki




I am currently a master's student in Center of Advanced Spatial Analysis in University College London. My main research interest lies in the agent-based human behavior modeling in the urban environment. In particular, I am interested in how music can enhance the human interaction, and contribute to urban reviatalization through the community building. My bachelor degree is in Physics from Kyoto University. Before starting my master's course, I worked at MIT Digital Currency Initiative to address Open Music Initiative. I am also a co-founder of Echowe, spin-off from OMI Summer Lab, where we attempt to reward the musicians automatically when their music is used for sampling through blockchain. My CV is here.
