I am currently a master's student in Center of Advanced Spatial Analysis in University College London. My main research interest lies in the agent-based human behavior modeling in the urban environment. Specifically, I attempt to combine the rule-based behavior modeling and the data-based behavior modeling to understand pedestrian movement in the city. And, I am also interested in how music can enhance the human interaction in urban context. I am doing my best (really?) to seek the intersection between these seemingly two different interests. My bachelor degree is in Physics from Kyoto University. Before starting my master's course, I worked at MIT Digital Currency Initiative to address Open Music Initiative. My CV is here.
